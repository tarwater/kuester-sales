About us:

Kuester Sales has been in business since 1939 serving the construction industry as manufacturer representatives. Direct manufacturer reps for products such as Louvers, Sunscreens, Grilles, Flagpoles, Stair nosings, Architectural Gratings & Mats, Corner Guards, Wall Guards & Wall Covering, Dock Bumpers, Athletic Flooring, Aluminum Ladders and Roof Hatches.

Our firm is capable of everything from design inception, working with architects and owners, providing custom detailing and engineering, direct quotes to contractors and overseeing all facets of our products lines.
